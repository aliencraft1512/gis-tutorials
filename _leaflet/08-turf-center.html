--- title: Intro to Leaflet subtitle: "GIS Analysis: Turf Center" description: This is an example of the turf.centerOfMass() function, which is being called on every county in the country. The resulting points are added to the map as individual L.circleMarker() features added to an L.geoJSON() feature. The radius is the winning vote percentage of the 2016 presidential race times the county population (divided by 60,000 to make them legible on the map). This example also highlights the vastly different maps that can be made from the same dataset. --- {% include leaflet/basic.js %} {% include leaflet/basemaps.js %} {% include leaflet/turf-centerOfMass.js %}
